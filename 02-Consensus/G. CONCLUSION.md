# Conclusion  
## Consensus Is the Strongly Agreed-Upon “Social Contract”

Bitcoin consensus is not only about technology — it is also a digital social contract.

Everyone who uses :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} agrees to these rules because those rules are precisely what give Bitcoin its value.

---

If someone attempts to change the fundamental rules and agreements inside Bitcoin, they would need to convince millions of people around the world to voluntarily download new software that could potentially destroy the value of their own assets.

Economically, that would be equivalent to collective self-destruction.

---

Bitcoin survives because consensus is enforced by:

- Mathematics
- Economic incentives
- Independent nodes
- Decentralized verification
- And voluntary agreement among participants

No king, government, corporation, miner, or developer can unilaterally rewrite the rules.

The network only changes when the overwhelming majority of participants willingly accept the change.

That is why Bitcoin consensus is powerful:

> It transforms trust in institutions into trust in rules.
